How to run brstm maker.
1. Download the maker: https://github.com/komaano/mkw-brstm-creator
2. Download ffmpeg and get the exe and not snapshots: https://ffmpeg.org/download.html#build-windows
3. make a folder in your C:\Program Files(x86)\ drive called ffmpeg and place all the contents in there. http://prntscr.com/vrzzk1
4. Go to windows search and search "environmental variables" https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/349771669243559939/782365381863800842/unknown.png
5. Locate : https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/349771669243559939/782365606489358367/unknown.png and hit "edit"
6. https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/349771669243559939/782365692740501514/unknown.png if you at this screen click new and 
add: https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/349771669243559939/782365914511048704/unknown.png
7.click okay and keep clicking okay until closed.
8. open command prompt and type "ffmpeg" and if you see
https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/349771669243559939/782364224617840650/unknown.png
then it works.
